STVEP (Strengthened Technical-Vocational Education Program) is a special program for children who are interested in
taking a course in coding, technology, and computer repair.

In STVEP, we teach our students two courses; Programming and CSS. In programming, students will be taught to use computers and
learn to code different coding languages. While in CSS, we teach students computer repairs and troubleshooting.

In past years, our well-known STVEP was originally known as SOF, otherwise known as School Of The Future. It was first developed to meet the needs of the modern world to implement and teach us Filipinos to learn and catch up to other countries in terms of technology.
